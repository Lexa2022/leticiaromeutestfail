The 80's

The 1980s were marked by several events. We would like to highlight here the success of the Walkman. First device that allowed you to have your favorite music wherever you were. This little device marked stories and memories. If you were born between the 80's and 90's you certainly had one of these.

The 90's

In the early 90's the Walkman was still one of the most talked about machines and best-selling when Sony launched the Discman. However, the audience that was most affected were students with the intention of using it to study with audiobooks.

The 2000's

In the early 2000s, the launch of MP3 began a small major evolution. The device had a USB memory card slot and a small black and white display, some with yellow backlighting. So it was no longer necessary to have the tapes or CDs with you. This saved space and made the product light and easy to carry.

The 2010's

At the beginning of 2010, MP3 was still widely used, when the MP4 was released. The device supported a larger capacity of USB memory card, had a color display and it was possible to watch small video clips on its screen.
